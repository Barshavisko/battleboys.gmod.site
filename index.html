The Battle Begins

The prisoners of a local prison have been let out of their cells, and they are angry! Fight against other gangs inside the prison area before prison reinforcements arrive to stop the fight, can you get the highest kill count?... will you teach that asshole a lesson?... Go for it!

Show your power

Show your colors to the guards, beat the hell out of everyone and win the round! There's no life limits, no microtransactions and no unbalancing! Go run amuck!

Show your colors

You will level up for every kill you get inside a round, your level can be used to unlock new cosmetical features, show off your true self from the orange prison rags you wear!
