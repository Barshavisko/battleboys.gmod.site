The Battle Begins

Battleboys (currently in alpha) is a small hectic team deathmatch gamemode for Garry's Mod, players are split into two gangs, The Kings and the Brotherhood. There are no starter weapons except for the shivs that the prisoners have crafted, but guns can be found around the prison, find them and you can rule the prison. Rounds start and last for a total of 5 minutes, less time will be used if a gang reaches the score limit and wins. If noone reaches the limit, the round will end when reinforcements arrive.

The prisoners of a local prison have been let out of their cells, and they are angry! Fight against other gangs inside the prison area before prison reinforcements arrive to stop the fight, can you get the highest kill count?... will you teach that asshole a lesson?... Go for it!

The gamemode is currently still in development, stay in contact with us and check our update log, over time we will show updates of new and changed content within the gamemode.

Show your power

Show your colors to the guards, beat the hell out of everyone and win the round! There's no life limits, no microtransactions and no unbalancing! Go run amuck!

Show your colors

You will level up for every kill you get inside a round, your level can be used to unlock new cosmetical features, show off your true self from the orange prison rags you wear!
